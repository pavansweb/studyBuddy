Chapter 7: Climate

Question 1: India experiences which type of climate?

Show Answer
The correct answer is Tropical Monsoon climate.

Question 2: The Deccan Plateau gets less rainfall than the Western Ghats because:

Show Answer
The correct answer is it lies in the rainshadow area of the Western Ghats.

Question 3: What causes the winter rain along the Coromandel Coast?

Show Answer
The correct answer is Retreating monsoon.

Question 4: Which of the following is NOT a source of rainfall for Punjab and Haryana?

Show Answer
The correct answer is Retreating monsoon.

Question 5: Which of the following factors does not affect the climate of a place?

Show Answer
The correct answer is Presence of vegetation.

Question 6: Western disturbances become active in northern India during which season?

Show Answer
The correct answer is Winter.

Question 7: The summer monsoon winds blow in which direction?

Show Answer
The correct answer is South-West.

Question 8: Which of the following is good for the cultivation of tea and jute?

Show Answer
The correct answer is Kalbaisakhi.

Question 9: Where does the Arabian Sea branch join the Bay of Bengal branch?

Show Answer
The correct answer is In the Sahyadris.

Question 10: What causes the 'October Heat'?

Show Answer
The correct answer is High temperature and humidity.

Question 11: Which state in India is the first to experience the onset of the monsoon and the last to see its withdrawal?

Show Answer
The correct answer is Kerala.

Question 12: Why do the interior parts of the Deccan remain dry during the Retreating monsoon?

Show Answer
The correct answer is Because they lie in the rainshadow of the Western Ghats.

Question 13: Climate of India is strongly influenced by ............... .

Show Answer
The correct answer is Monsoon winds.

Question 14: Which of the following factors cause the rise in the temperature of mainland of India in the month of March?

Show Answer
The correct answer is Northward migration of the Sun from the equator.

Question 15: Which of the following areas get rainfall from the Retreating monsoon?

Show Answer
The correct answer is East Coast of India.
